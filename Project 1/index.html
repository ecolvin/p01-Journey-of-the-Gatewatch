Welcome to the Blind Eternities, the dimension in between all of the planes of the Multiverse. You are a member of the Gatewatch, a group of planeswalkers charged with protecting the multiverse and its many planes. There are many planes that need your help, but as powerful as you are, you can only help them one at a time. Which would you like to choose first?

Ravnica

Ravnica is peaceful now that the guildpact has been reinstated, however, an evil planeswalker looks to threaten this peace. You must go and stop him before he turns the guilds on each other again.
